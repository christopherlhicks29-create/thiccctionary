Foreword Journal

A private brain-dump space for foreword notes. Whatever lands here gets stored in your browser's local storage. Doesn't have to be polished. Doesn't have to be coherent. Just dump what's in your head about why this project exists, why three c's, what the joke is, what got you to start.

All notes stay on this device — nothing is sent to a server. When you want Claude to capture them into print-exclusive.json, hit "Copy all notes" and paste into a chat session.
