Role-Scope Alignment Minutes

Convened by the Director of Editorial Operations with the Circulation Manager, 2004 to Present

Maintained by the Director of Editorial Operations · Convened as Warranted · Alignment Pending

The Director of Editorial Operations convenes a role-scope alignment meeting with the Circulation Manager whenever, in her assessment, or in the assessment of Human Resources, the role has room to grow. The Circulation Manager attends each one holding a printed copy of his position description. Meetings convened since 2004: 11. Cumulative meeting time: 61 minutes. Alignment remains pending.

Role-Scope Alignment · No. 11 · Convened 12 March 2026

Meeting opened 10:00. The Circulation Manager attended, holding a printed copy of the position description. The Director outlined an opportunity to reimagine the circulation function for a digital-first landscape. The Circulation Manager produced the document. No remarks. Adjourned 10:04.

Minutes kept by the Director
Hi all! Great session with Jon today, really valuable alignment energy in the room. We are going to parking-lot the reimagining conversation for Q1 2027 while we gather stakeholder input. Jon's clarity about his role continues to be such a strength! C.

Status: Alignment Pending · Q1 2027

Role-Scope Alignment · No. 10 · Convened 9 September 2022

Meeting opened 10:00. The Director proposed that the Circulation Manager "take ownership" of the publication’s newsletter. The Circulation Manager produced the document and indicated Section 4, which concerns newsstand returns. The newsletter is not a newsstand. Adjourned 10:03.

Minutes kept by the Director
Hi all! Jon raised a thoughtful process question about the newsletter opportunity and we agreed the scope needs more definition before we align. Adding "newsletter ownership model" to the Q3 parking lot. Great meeting! C.

Status: Scope Needs Definition

Role-Scope Alignment · No. 9 · Convened 14 February 2022

Meeting opened 10:00. Convened at the request of Human Resources regarding the outstanding Form 12-B (surname). The Circulation Manager produced the document. Form 12-B does not appear in it. Adjourned 10:02. The shortest alignment on record.

Minutes kept by the Director
Hi all! We connected on the Form 12-B question and I want to honor that Jon is technically correct, which is a kind of correct we really value here. HR has agreed to list the surname field as "in progress." Let me know if you need anything else! C.

Status: Surname In Progress · Since 2004

Role-Scope Alignment · No. 8 · Convened 4 June 2019

No meeting was convened. Entered into the file by the Director, out of cadence: during the June flood, the Circulation Manager, unprompted, moved the entire 1974-1989 archive to the third floor by hand truck in one morning, then returned to the loading dock without informing anyone. When asked afterward why, he indicated Section 7: "safeguarding of undistributed stock in transit." The archive is not stock. The Circulation Manager stated the archive "was not distributed, and was in transit." No further remarks were obtained.

Minutes kept by the Director
Hi all! I just want this ON THE RECORD because Jon will not let me give him the commendation (filing the acceptance form is, and I quote, "not in the PD"). The archive is dry because of him. We are all still talking about it. Jon, if you read these: thank you! C.

Status: Commendation 2019-03 · Unaccepted

Role-Scope Alignment · No. 7 · Convened 11 January 2018

Meeting opened 10:00. The Director presented a draft revision of the position description, prepared by an outside consultant, expanding the role to "Audience Circulation & Growth." The Circulation Manager read it in full, in silence, for eleven minutes. He then produced the original and placed the two side by side. The revision was withdrawn. Adjourned 10:15. The longest alignment on record.

Minutes kept by the Director
Hi all! We took a big swing today and I am so proud of the conversation. The revised PD needs another pass to make sure it honors the strengths of the current framework, which Jon knows extremely well. Parking-lotting for now. C.

Status: Revision Withdrawn
