The Editorial Board

The people who maintain the catalogue, file the objections, and answer the letters. Most do not pick up the telephone.

Bertram Whitmore

Founder & Publisher

Founded the enterprise in 1974, ostensibly from a Manhattan office that rented for sixty-two dollars a month, fully serviced. Has not visited the offices since 1991. Currently aboard the Margaret IV, latitude undisclosed. Drafts memos on a typewriter, sends them by post. Believes the publication peaked in 1986.

Column · From the Boat (irregular)

Bartholomew Whitmore

Editor-in-Chief · Senior Cataloguer

17 years at the publication. Wrote the original style guide. Believes the catalogue should have stopped accepting submissions in 2014. Has filed objections to 47% of all entries since 2019. Maintains a stapled copy of Girth Quarterly, Vol. 3, No. 2 in his desk drawer.

Column · Filed Replies (Mailbag, weekly)

Eliza Hartwell

Staff Writer

Sits by the window. Maintains a small notebook. Has counted three hundred and twelve fire hydrants in a six-block radius. Files what she sees, with restraint.

Column · Field Notes (lists, weekly)

Theodore (“Teddy”)

Junior Cataloguer

Eighteen months into his probation, of which he is unaware. Speaks Latin where possible. Has refiled the Saturn V brief, with citations. The dispute continues.

Column · Latinate Footnotes (occasional)

Margaret (“Margie”) Whitmore-Reuss

Society Correspondent

Files from a vineyard, generally. Currently Bruges; was Monaco. Niece of the founder. Catalogues girth at the gala, girth in the cellar, girth on the parade route.

Column · The Margie Column (when she returns calls)

Spider

Foreign Correspondent

A pseudonym, by all accounts. Bart maintains Spider is his brother-in-law Reginald, who has not returned from Jaipur. The expense reports arrive on time, correctly formatted, postmarked from rotating cities. The Senior Cataloguer is keeping a list.

Column · Spider Dispatches (one paragraph, irregular)

Hugh

Voiceover & Reels

Records the audio for the reels and the cartoons. Speaks slowly. Has been described as “documentary register, never winks.” Off-camera in all respects.

Reels narration (daily) · Cartoons (occasional)

The Margaret IV remains at sea. The Senior Cataloguer remains at his desk.
