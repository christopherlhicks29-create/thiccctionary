Editorial Decline

We do not rate humans here.

The Thiccctionary is a catalog of objects, vehicles, and produce. Bodies belong to their owners. Try a fire hydrant.

About this apparatus

The classifier reads the image, weighs it against our editorial standards (silhouette, ratio, character, presence), and returns a rating from 1 to 10 with commentary in the dictionary register.

It refuses photographs of people. This is not a body-rating tool. The rule is the brand promise.

You may rate up to 10 photographs per day at no cost. The limit resets at midnight UTC.
