# Cartoon test batch (2026-05-31, late evening)

These 3 panels are an experiment in a new cartoon framing after the prior batches were rejected for being "too inside-jokey" / "none of them make sense" / "off on all of them."

## What changed in the brief

Previous batches centered on cast members + office canon (the green cabinet, JAIPUR 0530, the Saturn V dispute, the parking lot poster). Strangers had no entry point. This batch flips it:

- The OBJECT is the joke (thiccc thing + comic scale)
- The CHARACTER is incidental, present only as a foreground reactor for scale
- The CAPTION is a dry, factual stat that lands the absurdity cold

Tests the "Onion benchmark" from `humor_balance` memory: "Area Man Passionate Defender of What He Imagines Constitution To Be." No prior context required.

## Panels

1. **test-01-parmigiano-wedding** - A man pushes a 4ft-diameter wheel of parmesan on a dolly toward a wedding tent. Caption: "A single wheel of Parmigiano-Reggiano, aged thirty-six months, weighs approximately one hundred ninety pounds. The wedding party had requested two."

2. **test-02-atlantic-giant-pumpkin** - Four men strain to lift a giant pumpkin onto a flatbed at a county fair. Caption: "The 1973 grand champion at the Topsfield Fair weighed in at one thousand two hundred forty-seven pounds. Six men were required to move it from the scale to the truck."

3. **test-03-cooling-tower-photographer** - A pinkie-sized press photographer photographs a nuclear cooling tower that fills the panel. Caption: "A natural-draft cooling tower at full load releases approximately twenty-five thousand pounds of water vapor per minute. The photographer below is five feet ten."

## Stranger-readability test

For each, the reader doesn't need to know any of: Bertram, Bart, Constance, Eliza, Teddy, Margie, Spider, Hugh, the green cabinet, the Saturn V feud, or any inside lore. The joke survives stripping the entire cast canon. Each panel could appear in a regional Sunday paper next to a Family Circus and read as a satire about scale.

## Bonus: catalog hook

None of these subjects are in the catalog yet. If any of the 3 lands, the panel can pair with a future entry for that subject (Parmigiano-Reggiano, Atlantic Giant pumpkin, natural-draft cooling tower).
