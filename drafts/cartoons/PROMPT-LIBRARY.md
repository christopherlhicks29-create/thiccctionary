## Format used

```
[N] Title
Scene: <one-paragraph description for nano_banana_pro>

```

## Approved-shape candidates

[1] Hoover Dam Worker
Scene: a hard-hat worker steps back to admire the curving concrete face of Hoover Dam. The dam fills the entire upper half of the panel. He is the size of a fingernail at the base.
Caption: "Hoover Dam contains six million six hundred thousand tons of concrete. The construction crew of 1933 was estimated at twenty-one thousand."

[2] Container Ship at Port
Scene: a single port crane operator, dwarfed by a freshly arrived 24,000-TEU container vessel. The ship fills the panel; the crane operator's cab is a tiny square against the hull.
Caption: "The Ever Ace, classed Megamax-24, displaces two hundred thirty-five thousand tons fully loaded. She carries twenty-four thousand of these."

[3] Sequoia Trunk
Scene: three forestry surveyors with measuring tape stand at the base of a giant sequoia. The trunk fills the entire panel left to right.
Caption: "The General Sherman, calculated by volume, is the largest single-stem tree on Earth. Twenty-five feet across at the base."

[4] Tortoise on the Lawn
Scene: a suburban homeowner in slippers, holding a coffee, looks down at an enormous Galapagos tortoise that has wandered into his front yard. The tortoise is the size of a love seat.
Caption: "An adult Galapagos tortoise can exceed five hundred pounds. They are not native to the suburbs of Tampa."

[5] Brisket on the Smoker
Scene: a backyard pitmaster lifts the lid of an offset smoker to reveal a single comically enormous beef brisket, far larger than the rack. The brisket steams.
Caption: "A USDA Prime full packer brisket can exceed twenty pounds prior to trim. Some butchers will not sell one without a forklift waiver."

[6] Cathedral Bell
Scene: two bellringers stand inside the bell chamber of a great cathedral. The bronze bell hangs above them, easily the size of a Volkswagen Beetle.
Caption: "The Petersglocke at Cologne Cathedral weighs twenty-four tons. It is rung on five occasions per year."

[7] Stack of Tires
Scene: a man stands next to a single Caterpillar 797F mining truck tire. The tire is taller than he is.
Caption: "A 63-inch off-the-road tire weighs approximately twelve thousand pounds. Replacement requires a crane."

[8] Whale Surfaces
Scene: a small wooden sailboat in the foreground, two sailors visible at the rail. A blue whale surfaces alongside, longer than the boat.
Caption: "An adult blue whale measures up to ninety-eight feet. The whale, in this case, is six feet longer than the boat."

[9] Wedding Cake
Scene: two confused pastry assistants stare at a seven-tier wedding cake on a rolling cart. The top tier is at eye level for a tall man.
Caption: "The 1947 Royal Wedding cake weighed five hundred pounds. It was shipped from Glasgow in seven separate crates."

[10] Library Book
Scene: a librarian on a step stool reaches for a single oversized atlas on a high shelf. The book is the size of a coffee table.
Caption: "The Klencke Atlas, presented to Charles II in 1660, measures six feet tall when opened. It is consulted twice a decade."

[11] Combine Harvester
Scene: a farmer in overalls stands at the side of his John Deere X9 combine, dwarfed by the cutting header that extends thirty feet across the panel.
Caption: "A modern combine harvester can clear a quarter section of wheat in a single afternoon. The header alone weighs twelve thousand pounds."

[12] Wedding Ham
Scene: a buffet table sagging under the weight of a single jamon iberico leg the size of a small dog. Two guests inspect it.
Caption: "A whole jamon iberico de bellota cures for four years and weighs eighteen pounds. The reception had requested twelve."

[13] Sushi Tuna
Scene: a Tsukiji fish market floor, a single 600-pound bluefin tuna laid out on the ice, four buyers in rubber boots examining it with flashlights.
Caption: "The 2019 Toyosu auction record was set by a 612-pound bluefin tuna that sold for three million one hundred thousand dollars."

[14] Pipe Organ
Scene: a small organist at the console of a great cathedral pipe organ. The largest pipes rise behind him to the ceiling, fifty feet up.
Caption: "The Boardwalk Hall Auditorium Organ has thirty-three thousand one hundred fourteen pipes. The longest is sixty-four feet."

[15] Sumo Wrestler
Scene: a tiny suit-and-tie referee in the foreground; behind him, a massive sumo wrestler at full mawashi stance. The wrestler easily outmasses the referee five to one.
Caption: "An ozeki-class sumo wrestler can exceed four hundred pounds. The mawashi alone is thirty feet of cotton."

[16] Boeing Engine
Scene: a ground crew technician in an orange vest stands beside the inlet of a Boeing 777-300ER engine. He can walk into the engine without ducking.
Caption: "The General Electric GE90-115B has an intake fan twelve feet five inches in diameter. It is the largest commercial jet engine in production."

[17] Pizza Pie
Scene: a family-style restaurant table; a single 36-inch pizza fills the entire frame, an awed family of four behind it.
Caption: "A thirty-six inch pizza pie weighs approximately fourteen pounds. Most restaurants require one hour notice."

[18] Crane Boom
Scene: a small foreman at the base of a tower crane on a construction site, looking up. The jib extends across the entire upper half of the panel.
Caption: "A Liebherr 81 K.1 has a thirty-six meter jib at radius. The counterweight is calculated to seven decimal places."

[19] Wine Bottle
Scene: a sommelier with white gloves carefully tilts an enormous wine bottle, easily three feet tall, into a glass. The label reads simply 'NEBUCHADNEZZAR.'
Caption: "A Nebuchadnezzar holds the equivalent of twenty standard bottles. It is named for the king who built the Hanging Gardens."

[20] Cattle Show Bull
Scene: a county fair show ring. A handler in a white shirt stands next to a single Chianina bull, the bull's shoulder at the man's eye level.
Caption: "The Chianina is the largest cattle breed in the world. A mature bull stands six feet at the withers and can exceed three thousand five hundred pounds."
