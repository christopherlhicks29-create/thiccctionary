## How to use this list

When pitching:
1. Pick one outlet from the highest-applicable tier.
2. Look up the named writer's *recent* article that's relevant (don't pre-cache — it ages out).
3. Use the angle in this list as a starting frame; tailor to the recent article.
4. Use one of the drafts in `outreach/drafts/` as the template.
5. Update Status above to "Pitched-YYYY-MM-DD" with sent date.
6. If they reply, update to "Replied" and follow up.
7. If they publish, update to "Featured" and add the URL.
