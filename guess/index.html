How this works

A new plate is pulled from the archive once a day, always at least a week removed from anything currently featured, so today's homepage entry can't spoil it. You get five guesses. Every miss burns off some of the fog and unlocks one more clue, in order: pronunciation, filing category, the first definition, then the image clears entirely. One attempt per day; come back tomorrow for the next plate.
